# HRMS 5-Minute Demo Video Script

**Title**: Next-Gen HRMS System Demo

**[0:00 - 0:30] Introduction & Login**
*   **Visual**: Screen showing the sleek login page.
*   **Audio**: "Welcome to our comprehensive HRMS platform built on the MERN stack. Today I'll walk you through how this system simplifies employee and HR operations. Let's start by logging in as an Administrator."
*   **Action**: Enter admin credentials and click Login.

**[0:30 - 1:15] Admin Dashboard & Employee Directory**
*   **Visual**: The Dashboard displaying quick stats (Total Employees, Leaves, etc.).
*   **Audio**: "Upon login, the Admin dashboard provides a bird's-eye view of organizational metrics. From the sidebar, let's navigate to the Employee Directory."
*   **Action**: Click on 'Employees' in the sidebar. Show the list of employees.
*   **Audio**: "Here, HR and Admins can view, edit, and add new staff members. Role-based access ensures that managers see what they need, while employees only see their own data."

**[1:15 - 2:00] Attendance & Leave Management**
*   **Visual**: Navigate to Attendance, then to Leave.
*   **Audio**: "Time tracking is a breeze. Employees can clock in and out right from their portal. Moving over to Leave Management, employees submit applications that instantly appear for Managers or HR to approve or reject."
*   **Action**: Demonstrate clicking the 'Apply Leave' button and filling out the modal form. Show a pending leave being approved.

**[2:00 - 2:45] Performance & Goal Tracking**
*   **Visual**: Navigate to Performance.
*   **Audio**: "Continuous feedback is the key to growth. Managers can assign goals or KPIs directly to employees. Employees can mark progress and write self-reviews, fostering transparency."
*   **Action**: Click 'Assign Goal' or show an existing goal card.

**[2:45 - 3:30] Payroll & Expenses**
*   **Visual**: Click on Payroll, then Expenses.
*   **Audio**: "Handling finances is secure and automated. In the Payroll module, HR can generate monthly payslips with exact deductions and taxes computed. Furthermore, employees can submit expense claims with receipt uploads using our Cloudinary integration."
*   **Action**: Show the Payroll table and then click 'Submit Claim' on the Expenses page.

**[3:30 - 4:15] Recruitment / ATS Module**
*   **Visual**: Navigate to Recruitment.
*   **Audio**: "Lastly, our built-in Applicant Tracking System allows administrators to post open jobs. Candidates can view these postings dynamically and apply with their resumes."
*   **Action**: Show an open job posting and the 'Apply Now' button view.

**[4:15 - 5:00] Conclusion & Outro**
*   **Visual**: Return to the Dashboard and perform Logout.
*   **Audio**: "This entire platform is built using React and Node.js for high performance and scalability. It provides everything a growing company needs out-of-the-box. Thank you for watching this quick tour of the HRMS."
*   **Action**: Complete the system logout, returning to the login screen. Fade to black.
